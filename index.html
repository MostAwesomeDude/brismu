Work in Progress

This is the landing page for brismu: a relational interpretation of Lojban, a small book which provides logical rules for manipulating Lojban text.

I am still in the process of writing the book. The first few phases are complete; currently, the book consists of a collection of notes for logicians and Lojbanists in English and a formally-verified collection of proofs about Lojban in Metamath. When complete, the notes will be a proper book which acts as an inspiration and guide for the formally-proven rules. I welcome contributions to both collections, and give credit to proof authors.
